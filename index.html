Welcome to All About CR7

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the greatest footballers of all time, known for his incredible skill, athleticism, and dedication to the sport. This website provides an in-depth look at his career and personal life.

About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal. He started his career at Sporting CP before moving to Manchester United in 2003, where he gained international fame. Ronaldo has won numerous awards, including five Ballon d'Or trophies, and has played for top clubs like Real Madrid and Juventus. Off the field, Ronaldo is known for his philanthropy and business ventures.
